Naritiv is an advertising platform that helps adapt traditional advertising firms and brands to the Gen-Z demographic by mastering the engagement of micro-content, specifically on Snapchat.

I spearheaded the creative storyboarding for unique Snapchat ad campaigns with budgets exceeding $750,000. Feature clients included Disney Studios, FreeForm (ABC Family), NFL, and Nike. I analyzed Snapchat engagement data and discovered insights for Fox Broadcasting and Coca-Cola to determine the value of unprecedented Snapchat advertising campaigns that received millions of views.

Naritiv has amassed a network of 300+ Snapchat stars and influencers — collectively seeing over 2 billion branded views on the platform. Naritiv connects those influencers with brands who are looking to set up campaigns on Snapchat and target a younger demographic.
