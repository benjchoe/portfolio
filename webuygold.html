The Year 2100 ft. 21 Savage

Alongside a team of five, we launched WeBuyGold's first Instagram animated series with Atlanta rapper, 21 Savage. Our Instagram account grew to and peaked at 150,000 followers, with over 13 million views across the series in only five weeks.

I spearheaded the social media growth strategy and produced promotional media alongside influencers that reached an audience of over 76 million people in our target demographic. Our network of social media influencers included @worldstar, @complex, @tank.sinatra, @satan, @shitheadsteve, @lmao and over 50 more partners.

Preparing for the next series, I storyboarded and pitched animated show concepts to hip-hop artists including Gucci Mane, A$AP Rocky, Big Sean, Rich Chigga & more.
