ben choe

inline digital
founder, ceo
june 2018 - present

Inline is redefining the wholesale process to automate, digitize and expedite previously manual and outdated processes in any industry in which physical B2B transactions occur. By drastically reducing time spent on outdated processes (ex. handwritten orders, manual data entry, and human generated analytics via Excel) and offering formerly inaccessible data insights, brands and retailers will be able to conduct more data-driven and efficient wholesale cycles through a centralized two-sided platform.

While working at Alexander Wang’s market week (a time period where wholesale buyers travel to attend showroom appointments and plan purchases for an entire season), Ben quickly noticed the antiquated nature in which sales were conducted. Even at one of the most successful luxury brands in the world, it was apparent that there were no digital tools being used to enhance the selling process or even keep track of sales in real time.

Inline is backed by the Sequoia Capital Scout Fund and Bread Ventures. Inline won first place at Tamid's university pitch competition, Tamid Tank 2018.
