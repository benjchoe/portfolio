Spark SC offers a well-constructed pipeline that supports entrepreneurs as they progress through the beginning stages of a venture (pre-seed). We also build the bridges between engineering, design and business talent at USC and industry professionals in LA. Last, we empower inner city high school students in LA to explore careers in the rapidly growing technology sector.

As Director of Development, I developed internal structure within committees and facilitated external relationships for exclusive sponsorships/fundraising to allow Spark SC to maximize productivity, allocate resources, and foster a growing network.

I was also the Director of Operations for 1000 Pitches, the largest student pitch competition in the world. As the Director of Operations, I led a team of six to collect over 1100 student pitches in 2-months, secure $33,000 in exclusive funding partnerships with Microsoft, Raytheon, & Riot Games, and hosted regular events with hundreds of budding entrepreneurs.
